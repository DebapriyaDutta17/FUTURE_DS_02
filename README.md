# 📊 Telco Customer Churn & Retention Intelligence Dashboard

A comprehensive Business Intelligence dashboard built using **Python, Pandas, and Plotly** to analyze customer attrition patterns, revenue impact, retention behavior, and service-level churn drivers in a telecommunications company.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the most critical challenges faced by subscription-based businesses. This project analyzes customer behavior using the classic *Telco Customer Churn Dataset* and transforms raw customer records into actionable business insights through an interactive executive dashboard.

### Key Business Questions Addressed:
* **Attrition Scale:** How many customers are leaving the company, and what is the overall churn velocity?
* **Financial Impact:** How much revenue is being lost due to churn?
* **Risk Segments:** Which customer demographics and cohorts (e.g., Senior Citizens) are at the highest risk?
* **Contract & Loyalty:** How do contract types and customer tenure brackets influence loyalty?
* **Service Drivers:** What role do value-added services like Tech Support and Online Security play in reducing churn?

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

* **Identify Drivers:** Pinpoint major underlying factors contributing to customer churn.
* **Quantify Revenue Impact:** Measure the financial leakages caused by customer attrition.
* **Cohort Analysis:** Evaluate customer retention patterns across contract types and tenure groups.
* **Service Optimization:** Discover feature/service associations that correlate with lower churn.
* **Strategic Support:** Enable data-driven, proactive retention strategies for the management team.

## 📂 Dataset & Data Preparation

### Dataset Overview
The project utilizes the **Telco Customer Churn Dataset**, which contains comprehensive records covering:
* **Demographics:** Gender, Senior Citizen status, Partners, Dependents.
* **Account Info:** Tenure, Contract type, Payment Method, Paperless Billing, Monthly Charges, Total Charges.
* **Services:** Phone, Multiple Lines, Internet (DSL/Fiber), Online Security, Online Backup, Device Protection, Tech Support, Streaming TV/Movies.

### 💡 Data Pipeline & Feature Engineering
1. **Data Cleaning:** Converted `TotalCharges` from object to numeric format and handled missing values gracefully.
2. **Standardization:** Cleaned and standardized text categories for service columns.
3. **Segmentation:** Grouped continuous customer `tenure` into intuitive cohort brackets (e.g., *0-1 year*, *1-2 years*, etc.).
4. **Financial Aggregations:** Engineered calculations to isolate and quantify monthly and cumulative revenue loss directly tied to churned flags.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

1. **Optimize the Onboarding Experience:** Focus engagement and onboarding success during the critical first 12 months where nearly half of the cohort churns (47.7%).
2. **Incentivize Contract Migrations:** Run targeted retention campaigns to convert high-risk Month-to-month accounts into stable multi-year agreements.
3. **Frictionless Billing Adjustments:** Review billing triggers and communication strategies surrounding the *Electronic Check* demographic to address the evident attrition anomaly there.
4. **Strategic Service Bundling:** Package high-retention services—like *Tech Support* and *Online Security*—into standard base tiers rather than keeping them as optional add-ons.
5. **Institutionalize Churn Metrics:** Track **Revenue Leakage due to Churn** as a foundational, board-level executive KPI.
